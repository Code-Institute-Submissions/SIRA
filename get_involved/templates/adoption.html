Apply

So you want to adopt a dog - what a wonderfull gift you can give to the world! Our Spanish rescue dogs are truly special, if you're willing to give them a chance, you will be changed forever.


Even though almost all of these dogs have come straight from the streets of Spain and only know that life, we still employ a strict application process. Our goal is to find the perfect family for them, and ensure they are the right fit for your family.


If you are interessed in adopting, the first step is to read the adoption guide, then contact us for an application form.

Home visit

After we receive your application, we schedule you for a home visit with one of our volunteers. If we don't have any volunteers near you, we arrange for a "virtual" vist, via Google Meet, Skype, Whatsapp Video Call, or Facetime with someone from our team. This helps is ensure it will be the perfect match!

Contract

Altough we don't have an adoption "fee", we do require a donation to reimburse us for vaccines, microchipping and transport. The donation we request is € 310 per dog. This donation must be paid when you sign and submit the adoption contract.

Transport

If you are adoption a dog that is in Spain; after recieving your payment, we start arranging transport. For your planning purposes, the transport timeline can range from 3 weeks to several months, depending on transportation availability, fundraising, etc. Please check with our adoption team to get more info on your specific pet.

If you are adopting a dog that is in your country; After recieving your payment, we will approve the adoption and the foster family will arrange a pick up date for your pet.

Together Forever

Members of our team will keep you constantly updated during your pet's transport. Then, we all just wait for your pet to arrive safely to you! And the rest is your happily-ever-after with your new rescue dog from Spain.
