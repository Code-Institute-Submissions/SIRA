About Us

Stichting I Respect Animals also know as SIRA is a non-profit orginazation in The Netherlands.

Sandra and Cees van Dijk together with Suzanne van der Hout, founded SIRA in November of 2016.

Our mission is to bring awerness and support to saving the forgotten, homeless petd of Spain.

SIRA accomplishes there mission through rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption through networking and volunteers.
